Document Upload & Verification

Upload clear photos of your identity documents and a recent selfie for verification
ℹ️

Important: All documents must be clear, valid, and not previously used for KYC verification.

Your face will be matched against your ID document using advanced facial recognition technology.

🪪 Front ID Document

Upload a clear photo of the front of your ID:

  • Ensure the entire document is visible and in focus
  • Good lighting is essential
  • Avoid glare or reflections
  • Accepted formats: JPG, PNG, PDF
  • Maximum file size: 10MB

🪪 Back ID Document

Upload a clear photo of the back of your ID:

  • Ensure the entire document is visible and in focus
  • Good lighting is essential
  • Avoid glare or reflections
  • Accepted formats: JPG, PNG, PDF
  • Maximum file size: 10MB

🤳 Selfie / Face Verification

Upload a recent selfie for facial verification:

  • Take a clear photo of your face looking straight at the camera
  • Good lighting - face should be clearly visible
  • Remove sunglasses and avoid shadows on face
  • Recent photo (taken within the last 3 months)
  • Must clearly match your ID document
  • Accepted formats: JPG, PNG
  • Maximum file size: 10MB
